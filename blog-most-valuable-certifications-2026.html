How to Study Efficiently

  • Practice exams are essential: Buy practice tests from Tutorials Dojo (AWS) or Whizlabs. Take timed practice exams weekly to identify weak areas
  • Hands-on labs: Theory alone does not pass certification exams. Build real projects. AWS, GCP, and Azure all offer free tier resources for practice
  • Study schedule: 1-2 hours daily for 8-12 weeks is more effective than cramming. Use spaced repetition (Anki flashcards) for technical concepts
